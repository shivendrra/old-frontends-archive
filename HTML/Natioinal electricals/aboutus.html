About Us

National Electricals started as manufacturing unit of automatic voltage stabilizer & voltage regulators in 1967 in Gorakhpur. The company was further promoted by Mr. Harihar Singh. Over the years, our constant dedication towards excellence has helped us attain expertise in production of various products like rectifiers & servo voltage stabilizers, Leading Suppliers of power transformers India & power control solutions.
